About Waffle Cabin


Founded in 1998

The newest company deciding to turn to food trucks is every skier's favorite: Waffle Cabin! Whenever you're skiing to the chairlift or heading in for a break, we all know you smell our freshly baked waffles from our well known cabins. I bet you wished you weren't skiing and could just sit and eat our waffles forever. Now, you will find Waffle Cabins not just on the ski slopes, but in town or places without ski mountains! Enjoy a fresh waffle made withreal ingredients today!

The founder of Waffle Cabins claims he was going to start the business with food trucks, but invested more money into building shacks on the ski slopes. Now he enjoys that his original idea and dream is coming true.
